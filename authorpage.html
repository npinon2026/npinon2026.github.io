Writing was one of the few things I was passionate about as a little girl, and it still is to this day.
As a child with a vivid imagination, I had a hard time coping with the fact that reality could never reach my expectations since no matter how hard I tried my dreams were no better than a fictional fairy tale.

It is through writing that I was able to bring to life those fairytales I created. I found joy in it and learned far more in the general aspects of life. (Surprisingly enough)
All in all, writing has had a huge impact on who I am as a person, and I am glad that little me didn't lose the opportunity to pick up a pen.
