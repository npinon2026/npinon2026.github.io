The Seven Lying Knights

By: ShushMyst

"For the land of Teyvat is perfect and true"
My dear child, they have lied to you.
For beneath the stars that light up the sky
Are the hidden truths among the lie

My dear child, have you heard of the fable?
About the seven knights that ruled the table
But don't be fooled by their innocent smiles
For as I said, it is all a lie

The pyro knight, whose passion burned bright
A fleeting sun that shines its own light
But have you heard of her painstaken cry?
That pierces upon the bloody night sky?

The electro knight, a being unpredictable
Among the rest, she stands so special
But a failure she is, for she has yet to see
That even she cannot last an eternity

The hydro knight, so pure and just
Morals of righteousness, a being you trust
But beneath her beauty, a dark loom stares
Even she cannot judge those unfair

The dendro knight, who knows no bounds
The secrets of the world, once lost now found
But what is the price of the secret she keeps?
Why is it, the ground continues to bleed?

The geo knight, whose decisions are set
A contract is forged, an agreement is met
And yet how are you sure you aren't defied?
By the man who's tied with the divine

The cryo knight, whose love is so gentle
A being adored by the nation she handles
Oh how cruel the world is, as it is all for naught
A love so pure, has been left to rot

The anemo knight, one who is free
A friend of time, a bard who flees
And yet past and future he may know
He has yet to reap what he has sow

My dear child, it is all a lie
Such as the stars that shine so bright
But look away, remain a fool
For the land of Teyvat is perfect and true
